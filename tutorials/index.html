simple1.py (ANSL)

this simple example just registers a variable, reads its value and then exit after a few seconds

simple2.py (ANSL)

this simple example just registers a variable for reading and another for writing. In fact we switch on the 'coffee machine' and watch its temperature ...

simple3.py (INA2000)

this simple example just registers a variable for reading and another for writing. In fact we switch on the 'coffee machine' and watch its temperature ... This is similar to simple2.py but we use a control running AR 3.x. ANSL is not available here and we change to good old INA2000
